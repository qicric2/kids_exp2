About Us

The Kiddies Party Expo is an expo designed to be a one-stop party solution! The expo allows parents to get all the necessary contact details, make bookings, take advantage of special kiddies Party Expo offers and essentially plan their kiddie's birthday party in one day! All this is done while their kiddies have fun in the Fun Zone and being part of the daily entertainment programe.
